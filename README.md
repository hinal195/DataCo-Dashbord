Supply Chain Sales Analysis Dashboard using Power BI
📌 Project Overview

The Supply Chain Sales Analysis Dashboard is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed in Power BI to transform raw supply chain data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard helps organizations monitor sales performance, product profitability, customer behavior, and shipping efficiency through interactive visualizations and dynamic KPIs.

The project follows a Star Schema data model, uses Power Query for data transformation, and leverages DAX measures for advanced calculations. It enables stakeholders to make informed decisions by providing a comprehensive view of business operations across multiple markets.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
Monitor product and category performance.
Understand customer purchasing behavior.
Evaluate shipping and delivery performance.
Identify high-performing markets and regions.
Provide interactive dashboards for data-driven decision-making.
